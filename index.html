BMW F90 M5 CS

The BMW F90 M5 CS is the pinnacle of performance and luxury in the M5 lineup. This high-performance sedan combines aggressive styling with track-ready capabilities to deliver an exhilarating driving experience.

  • Powered by a 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine
  • the M5 CS produces an astounding 627 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque
  • making it the most powerful M5 ever produced. With this immense power,
  • the M5 CS can sprint from 0 to 60 mph in just 2.9 seconds, putting it in supercar territory.
  • Its top speed is electronically limited to 190 mph, ensuring breathtaking speed on the open road.

The M5 CS features a host of enhancements over the standard M5 to improve performance and handling. It comes equipped with an upgraded suspension system, carbon-ceramic brakes, and a specially-tuned exhaust system that delivers a captivating sound. The exterior design is accentuated by carbon fiber elements, including the roof, front splitter, and rear diffuser, which not only enhance its sporty appearance but also contribute to weight reduction. Inside the cabin, the M5 CS offers a luxurious and driver-focused environment. It features lightweight M Carbon seats, Alcantara upholstery, and carbon fiber trim, providing a perfect balance between comfort and sportiness. The BMW F90 M5 CS is the ultimate expression of BMW's M performance division, combining blistering speed, precision handling, and opulent comfort in one remarkable package.
